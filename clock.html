Monday, April 19, XXXX

Local Clock Removed After Refusing To Advance

Municipal maintenance crews removed a public clock from the corner of Maple Street following reports that its display remained fixed at 2:17 p.m. for an extended period. According to city records, the device continued to function electrically, though no visible progression of time was observed.

Officials stated that multiple inspections were conducted prior to removal, all of which indicated the clock was operating within standard mechanical parameters. Despite this, residents noted that daily scheduling adjustments had already been made in response to its condition.

“It didn’t really change anything,” one passerby said. “We just started using other clocks.”
