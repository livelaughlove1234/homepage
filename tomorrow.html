Monday, April 19, XXXX

Man Claims He Remembers Tomorrow

A local resident has reported an ongoing ability to recall events prior to their occurrence. The individual has begun documenting these recollections in written form, several of which have later corresponded with observed community events. No formal investigation has been initiated at this time.

Neighbors describe the situation as unusual but non-disruptive, noting that the individual’s daily routines remain unchanged. According to those familiar with the case, the reports have not impacted local schedules or services.

“I don’t really see it affecting anything,” one resident stated. “He just seems to know things a little early.”

Subject during routine documentation of observed events.
